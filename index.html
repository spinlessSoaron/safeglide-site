What is safeGlide?

safeGlide is a personal toolkit for soaring pilots that reviews recorded flights for risky flying situations and tracks how your exposure to them evolves over a flying career. Point it at a single flight for a detailed debrief, or at your whole logbook to see the bigger picture.

🪂

Single-flight review

Load one IGC file and see the whole flight on an interactive map, with altitude / speed / risk timelines and a table of flagged moments and thermal climbs.

📈

Career trends

Aggregate the risk events from every flight you've logged and see how often they occur, and whether that's improving as your experience grows.

⛰️

Terrain-aware

Uses elevation data and a database of landable fields to reason about terrain proximity and whether a safe landing option is still within reach.

📱

Native apps

A GliderTrends app for macOS & iOS, plus an in-flight GliderCockpit companion — the same analysis, on your own devices.

How safeGlide works, and what to expect when you use it.

How it works

  1. Bring in your flights

    Start from local .igc files, or connect your WeGlide account and let safeGlide pull your own flights automatically. You can analyse one flight or your entire logbook.

  2. Automatic analysis

    Each flight's track is reconstructed and assessed for moments that carried elevated risk. Results are remembered between runs, so re-analysing a growing logbook is quick — only new flights are processed.

  3. Review the results

    Dive into a single flight on an interactive map with synced timelines, or step back and look at how your risk events trend across months and years of flying.

What safeGlide flags

Flagged moments are graded by severity so you can focus on what matters:

caution warning critical

At a high level, it watches for the classic soaring squeeze — being low and slow and close to the ground at the same time — and for situations where a safe landing field may no longer be within comfortable glide range. The goal isn't to second-guess any single decision, but to surface patterns you might not notice flight-to-flight.

Features to expect

  • Interactive flight map — your full track rendered in the browser, with risky segments highlighted in place.
  • Synced timelines — altitude, speed and risk level plotted along the flight, so you can scrub to any moment.
  • Episode & thermal table — a sortable list of every flagged moment and every detected climb, with the key numbers for each.
  • Career trend charts — risk events shown as rates over your accumulated flying experience, separated by category and by severity.
  • WeGlide integration — pull your own logged flights directly, no manual file wrangling.
  • Fast re-runs — previously analysed flights are cached, so updating your trends after a new flight takes seconds.
  • Multilingual — the interface and the written flight summary are available in English, German and French.
  • Native macOS & iOS apps — the trend analysis as a desktop and mobile app, plus an in-flight cockpit companion.

What you'll need

  • Flight logs — standard .igc files from your logger or flight computer.
  • A WeGlide account (optional) — only if you want flights pulled in automatically. WeGlide only lets you download your own flights.
⚠️ Not a certified safety device. safeGlide is a personal analysis project for post-flight review and self-study. It is not avionics, not certified, and must never be relied on for in-flight decision-making. Always fly within your own training, currency and judgement.
